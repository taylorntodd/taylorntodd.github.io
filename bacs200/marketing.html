A WOMAN'S PLACE

Allowing Women Feel at Home Again

A Women's Place has devoted themselves to empower victims and survivors of domestic abuse to heal and grow into strong women again. Through their services, women are able to plan, prepare, and protect themselves to feel safe. They offer a 24/7 hotline with live victum advocates to help leave an abusive relationship.

Accomplishments

  • 949 calls answered
  • 402 Adult Clients Served
  • 120 clients Recieve Employment Support
  • 981 Legal Advocacy Services Provided
  • 167 Community Presentations
