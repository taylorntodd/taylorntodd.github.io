Social Implications

<Homelessness has been an issue that seems to be gaining more popularity in recent years. In combination with these factors, homlessness is not a "one size fits all". In the United States of America, homelessness is classified as " “lacks a fixed, regular, and adequate nighttime residence, and if they sleep in a shelter designated for temporary living accommodations or in places not designated for human habitation." This definition is broad, but gives a good idea of how people can suffer from this issue.
